Smart Reading

Read with support, not alone

This section is designed to help you read more confidently. You can use guided reading pages to understand texts more clearly, notice important words and structures, and build stronger reading habits.

This is still a trial version.
As you use these reading pages, please think about:
1. Which supports help you the most?
2. What kinds of help are still missing?

What Smart Reading is for

  • Reading with support instead of reading alone
  • Understanding text more clearly
  • Noticing vocabulary and structure while reading
  • Building confidence step by step
  • Helping us learn what students still need
